Firefighter Robot, High School Computer Engineering Class, February – June 2011

During my grade 12 Computer Engineering class, me and a partner have designed and manufactured a firefighting robot. We began with only a schematic of the circuits we were to use given to us by the teacher. From there we designed the circuit boards, manufactured them using a multi-step process, drilled the holes and soldered the various components onto them. Once our boards were completed, we began programming the code that was to process the various sensor inputs and creating a searching pattern to take the robot around the maze. The process of creating and testing the robots was a very long one, and we have very tight deadlines to meet during the term. In the end, we created a fully functional, autonomous robot that is able to navigate a maze to find fire and put it out.
